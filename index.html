From Khatib station (NS14), take the Mandai Khatib Shuttle
From Choa Chu Kang station (NS4), take bus number 927
From Ang Mo Kio station (NS16), take bus number 138
From Woodlands (NS9) and Marisiling station (NS8), take bus number 926 (please note that this bus only operates on Sundays and PH)

Attractions

In case you're wondering what there is to see in our park, here's our map!

Start your journey at River Gems and marvel at the beauty of underwater wildlife in our exhibits.


Next, marvel at some of the largest freshwater species in the world, the giant freshwater stingray
and the Mekong giant catfish in our Mekong River aquarium.


Rounding off the River Gems, be in awe at the size of the critically endangered
Chinese sturgeons in the Yangtze river exhibit.


Enter the Giant Panda Forest, home to our resident Giant Pandas KaiKai and JiaJia.
Oh, and don't forget the Red Panda!


Finally, visit the world's largest freshwater aquarium, our Amazon Flooded Forest.


Activities

Participate in our Once Upon A River animal show and get up close and personal with our lovely animals! .


Be sure to go on the Amazon River Quest boat ride, do try and spot the elusive Jaguars!.
